Las propiedades del producto vectorial son las siguientes:

  1. u ⋅ (u × v) = 0
  2. v ⋅ (u × v) = 0
  3. ||u × v||2 = ||u||2||v||2 - (u ⋅ v)2
  4. u × v = -(v × u)
  5. u × (v × w) = u × v + u × w
  6. (u + v) × w = u × w + v × w
  7. α(u × v) = (αu) × v = u × (αv)
  8. u × 0 = 0 × u = 0
  9. u × u = 0
